We're thrilled to announce that RoCreate will be participating in the Winter Artisan Market this December!

## Event Details

**When:** Saturday, December 14th, 2025
**Time:** 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM
**Admission:** Free

## What to Expect

Come visit our booth and explore our latest collection of handcrafted jewelry, including:

- New winter-inspired necklace designs
- Delicate earrings perfect for the holiday season
- Custom bracelet sets
- One-of-a-kind statement pieces
- Special market-exclusive items

## Meet the Artist

This is a wonderful opportunity to meet face-to-face, see the pieces in person, and discuss custom orders for the upcoming year. We'll have plenty of pieces available for purchase, perfect for holiday gifts or treating yourself!

## Special Market Pricing

As a thank-you for supporting local artisans, we'll be offering special pricing on select items throughout the day. Plus, custom order consultations will receive a 10% discount when booked at the event.

We hope to see you there! Feel free to reach out with any questions at [Rochelle@rocreate](mailto:Rochelle@rocreate).
